AstroLife

Astrology Mobile App

About The Project

A comprehensive astrology application designed to provide users with detailed insights into their lives. From generating precise Kundalis to offering daily Panchang and personalized horoscope readings, AstroLife is your personal guide to the stars.

Key Features

  • Kundali Generation: Create accurate birth charts based on precise astrological calculations.
  • Daily Panchang: Access daily astrological information and auspicious timings.
  • Personalized Horoscopes: Get tailored readings and insights.
